About Sailstack

At Sailstack, we believe that AI should empower realtors, not replace them. With over 700 companies already offering AI-powered real estate technology solutions, the industry is rapidly evolving.

Our mission is to help realtors stay competitive by harnessing the power of AI agents to improve productivity and client service.

Research from JLL shows that more than 90% of C-suite leaders believe AI will change workforce operations, and 89% believe AI can solve major commercial real estate challenges. With nearly two-thirds of companies piloting AI use cases but facing a shortage of AI-skilled talent, there's a critical need for accessible AI training and consulting.

We provide consulting services for realtors of all tech literacy levels, offering personalized guidance and strategic implementation plans to integrate AI into your daily operations.
